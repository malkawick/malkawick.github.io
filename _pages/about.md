I am a first-year undergraduate student at the [University of Moratuwa](https://uom.lk/), pursuing a BSc. (Hons.) in Electronic and Telecommunication Engineering. Currently, I am maintaining a GPA of 4.0/4.0.

My technical interests span embedded systems, telecommunications, and hardware design. I am actively involved in several engineering initiatives:
* **Project Phoenix:** Developing the Ground Station User Interface and testing LoRA communications for a CubeSatellite high-altitude weather balloon.
* **Smart Navigation:** Designed an ESP32-based smart hiking pole integrating GPS and IMU sensors with a custom path-retracing algorithm.
* **Water Purification:** Collaborating on an engineering design project to create a low-cost, household water purifier using Electrocoagulation (EC) to address CKDu in Sri Lanka.

I am proficient in Python, C/C++, and MATLAB, and I am an active IEEE Student Member. Beyond engineering, I am a classical pianist and enjoy swimming.

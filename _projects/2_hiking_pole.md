**Duration:** Sept 2025 – Dec 2025  
**Team:** Group project (4 members)

Conventional navigation services often fail in extreme or remote environments. To solve this, our team designed an ESP32-based smart hiking pole integrating GPS and IMU sensors for reliable location tracking.

* **Path Retracing:** Developed a path-retracing algorithm utilizing logged GPS coordinates to generate return routes for users without relying on external cellular networks.
* **Hardware Integration:** Engineered a functional breadboard prototype, directly addressing technical challenges in signal acquisition and sensor integration.

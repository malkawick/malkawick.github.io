**Duration:** Oct 2025 – Present  
**Organization:** Electronic Club of UoM in collaboration with SEDS Mora

Project Phoenix is a CubeSatellite initiative aimed at high-altitude telemetry and communication. My technical contributions to the team include:

* **UI Development:** Developed the Ground Station User Interface (UI) for a high-altitude weather balloon using Python and HTML for real-time telemetry visualization.
* **Telemetry Testing:** Collaborated in the validation and testing of the LoRA-based communication sub-system to ensure reliable data transmission under extreme conditions.
